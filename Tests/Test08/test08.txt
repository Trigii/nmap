Description: Scan host with a valid and with blacklisted subject Organization and not known domain name.

Input:
	* list: blacklist.csv
	* dns-list: dns.csv

Output:
	* Information about the certificate
	* Self-signed: True
	* Verified: True
	* Validity: False (Expired)
	* Valid names: False
	* Blacklisted: True. Blacklisted subject and issuer (self-signed)
	* DNS resolved: False

Command: $ nmap -Pn -p443 10.0.3.149 --script untrustedX509certs.nse --script-args list=blacklist.csv dns-list=dns.csv -v -oN test08.out
