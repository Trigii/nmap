Description: Scan host with a valid and with not blacklisted certificate signed by a blacklisted CA (CN) and no Subject Alternative Names.

Input:
	* list: blacklist.csv
	* dns-list: dns.csv

Output:
	* Information about the certificate
	* Self-signed: False
	* Verified: True
	* Validity: True
	* Valid names: False
	* Blacklisted: True. Blacklisted CA.
	* DNS resolved: False

Command: $ nmap -Pn -p443 10.0.3.149 --script untrustedX509certs.nse --script-args list=blacklist.csv dns-list=dns.csv -v -oN test12.out
